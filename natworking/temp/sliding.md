## How to do a slide

I like to use:

* H1 for section titles
- H2 for slide titles
+ H3 for additional headings in slides

- convert from markdown to slides

```

pandoc [file].md -o [newName].pptx

How to convert this slide

pandoc myslides.md -o myslides.pptx
```
